About Stable 45.

Stable 45 is a website about the fictional Stable Lab 45 Center of Reserch and Development of Entertainment Technology and its inhabitants. It is primarily intended to entertain teenagers that like silly, whimsical scenarioes with stories and games, as well as provide information to facilitate roleplay within the setting.

Stable 45 also includes short sketch-stories as well as games that flesh out the scenario. The stories will have copies hosted on-site as well as links to Google Docs copies.

If you wish to contact me- the site's creator- about the site, you can reach me at travisuped@gmail.com. There's also a contact form to fill out the email for you here.
