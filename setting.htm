Settlements of Stable 45.

After the Pioneers of Stable 45 set off to find what lay beyond their sealed doors, some found places to settle and make their homes. Those homes grew over time and become villages.

Cookie Console - the first settlement. A cookie factory found near the site of the Stable Lab, partially underground. The Pioneers rebuilt it, and their homes were built around it.
